HackerCamp 2026 is ON!

Ready to unleash your inner hacker and outdoor adventurer? Join us for HackerCamp — a weekend-long getaway where tech and nature collide.

What’s HackerCamp?

HackerCamp is a weekend-long outdoor hacking adventure for all ages. You can think of it as a mini security conference, but with fresh air and sunlight! We’ll be teaching everything from Cryptography, to lock picking, and even fire starting. We'll also have board and card games to keep the fun going for those members of your party not interested in hacking. No experience? No problem! We welcome everyone from the tech-curious to the seasoned pros.

What can I expect?

It's all about unplugging, hacking, and hanging out with fellow hackers in the great outdoors. Whether you're into coding, cybersecurity, or just hanging by the campfire with your fellow digital rebels, there’s something for everyone. We’ve got everything you need to make this weekend one for the books – from good vibes to epic views, and enough hacker spirit to keep you going all weekend long.

Payson Lakes Campground

This year we’re excited to be setting up camp at Payson Lakes Campground near Loafer Mountain, a beautiful spot with lakes, Pines, and Quakies! Only an hour drive from Salt Lake. Escape the digital chaos and enjoy a true outdoor experience, with plenty of room to hack, chill, and enjoy the fresh air.
